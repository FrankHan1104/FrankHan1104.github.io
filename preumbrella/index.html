PRE-UMBRELLA

PRE-UMBRELLA is an innovative weather app that alleviates your weather-related worries. Our goal is to assist users in easily preparing for weather changes. Let's take a closer look at the key features of PRE-UMBRELLA: Early Notifications: PRE-UMBRELLA provides advance alerts for expected rain or snow. Receive notifications before your set time to ensure you're always prepared. Current and Tomorrow's Weather: Use the app to check both current and tomorrow's weather. Obtain essential information for planning your daily activities. User-Friendly Notification System: Users can customize notification times, ensuring they're ready for sudden weather shifts. Swift Alerts and Accurate Predictions: PRE-UMBRELLA offers swift and precise weather information, keeping you updated on the latest weather conditions. Reminders to Carry an Umbrella: Our app reminds you not to forget your umbrella, eliminating the risk of leaving it behind. PRE-UMBRELLA is your ideal companion, making your daily life more convenient and secure. Start your day without worrying about the weather, thanks to PRE-UMBRELLA, which prioritizes your convenience and always considers your needs with rapid and reliable weather updates.
